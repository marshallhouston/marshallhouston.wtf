beat 6. close.

let it burn.

we already made this mistake once.

  • us forest service, early 1900s. fight every fire.
  • decades of suppression. fuel stacks up.
  • small fires stop happening. catastrophic ones arrive.
  • nature was already running a process. we overrode it.

same pattern in our work.

  • we suppress. keep projects alive past usefulness.
  • can't say no. can't kill. can't sunset.
  • fuel stacks up. cognitive load. maintenance. drag.
  • the catastrophic fire is the reorg, the layoff, the collapse.

intellectual honesty.

look at what's there. admit what should burn. let it.
