new skill in the cosmic farmland: `/fart-sniffing-detection`. check it out here: [https://github.com/marshallhouston/cosmic-farmland/tree/main/plugins/cosmic-farmland/skills/fart-sniffing-detection](https://github.com/marshallhouston/cosmic-farmland/tree/main/plugins/cosmic-farmland/skills/fart-sniffing-detection).

aliased as `/ptvm` and `/prove-the-value-motherfucker`.

goal is to press hard on "do these changes actually provide value or are we just sniffing our own farts and calling it cologne?"

run it on a PR, across a range of commits, staged changes. it walks through each change and determines whether this is fluff or actual value. simplifies and focuses on what matters.

four levels to choose from.

- `whiff`: benefit of the doubt. only flag obvious dead weight.
- `sniff`: balanced. default. flag speculative defense and unused abstractions.
- `huff`: skeptical. must prove concrete value today, not hypothetical.
- `dutch-oven-yourselff`: max skepticism. anything not load-bearing. double f here to keep the pattern. lol
