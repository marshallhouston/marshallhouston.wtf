> Seamless transition from INM to Guided Reading…. the transition point should be marked by the increase in depth of CFUs.
>
> I'm struggling with translating my mindset into actions. I know that the approach I bring each morning is rooted in the empowerment of my students, but I haven't seen the results I expect from my students.
>
> The most difficult realization is that the results haven't happened BECAUSE of my teaching. I am learning and improving every single day, but I expect more out of myself than I've been able to give to my students. As I learn more about effective teaching, I find myself wishing that I would've implemented those strategies the day before….
>
> I keep coming back to the mindset versus actions thought.
>
> Here's what I know and believe….
>
> I believe that I am a creative person. I believe that I was hired because my creativity showed in my teaching. I believe that I will see significant student growth—both academically and personally—because TOGETHER my students and I will achieve.
>
> However, I've struggled throughout institute to translate these mindsets into action on a daily basis that leads to the type of student achievement that I expect. It is not enough for my students .
>
> Throughout this week, I've wrestled with the reality that if I fail as a teacher, I won't be the only person to fail. It will be my students too.
>
> Yet, I will not fail. We will achieve, and I cannot wait to share these successes with the world.
>
> The successes of my students will enable their empowerment that is already occurring in the community. These students—the young men and women who WILL create a better world for everyone through their actions—deserve the best from me every single moment.
>
> I will find a way. Together, we will achieve. No doubt.
