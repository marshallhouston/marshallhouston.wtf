future concern: as ideas, drafts, and posts grow, how do we keep things from becoming a mess? need a system for moving ideas through stages (idea -> draft -> post) and tracking what's happening.

## inspiration: rands in repose
https://randsinrepose.com/archives/better-faster-and-even-more/

rands uses a worklog.md per project as a session diary — what was investigated, what changed, decisions and rationale. gives future sessions context to pick up where you left off without re-explaining everything.

## things to figure out
- work logs: "here's what i'm doing now, here's how i move forward"
- idea lifecycle: how do things move from `_ideas/` to `_drafts/` to `_posts/`?
- keeping it accessible across sites and computers
- not over-engineering it upfront — let the system emerge from actual use (bff it)
