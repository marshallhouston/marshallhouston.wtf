title: make following easy
captured: 2026-03-22
---

a friend asked how to follow the site. there should be an obvious way to do that.

ideas:
- rss feed link in the nav or footer (jekyll generates one by default via jekyll-feed plugin)
- email subscription (buttondown, convertkit, substack-style embed?)
- clear "follow" or "subscribe" call to action on the homepage or about page
- social links if relevant (bluesky, linkedin)

keep it simple — one or two options, easy to find.
