## the kernel (2026-03-25)

two geometric tattoos that shape how i think and approach things.

### the two triangles (left and right inner forearms)

- left arm: equilateral triangle, point facing back toward me
- right arm: equilateral triangle, point facing away
- super simple geometric tattoos. and they carry so much.

what they represent:
- **duality.** change in, change out. receiving and projecting.
- **threes.** past, present, future. mind, body, spirit.
- **energies.** masculine and feminine. mom and dad. different orientations of the same shape.
- **sacred geometry.** triangles are strength. triangles are fascinating.
- **power line.** riding my bike back after getting them, looked down and saw a line of power running from right forearm to left. harnessing something. change, duality, threes, spiritual, sacred geometry, all flowing through.

### the circle with two lines of tangency (right shoulder)

- a circle. two straight lines meeting at a single point of tangency on the circle.
- told someone about it in berlin, playing pool at a hostel.

what it represents:
- **the circle is all possibilities.** across all space and time, all dimensions, everything.
- **the two lines are personal histories.** everyone's past, converging.
- **the point of tangency is right now.** the present moment. the exact point where we meet.
- we're in this present together on a single point in the time continuum circle and then it goes past. it moves on.
- i don't know what's been in someone's past but we all bring that. we're in this moment, it's electric, and then it goes on.

how has physically putting these ideas on my body shaped how i think? has it changed anything or just made existing thinking more concrete? this is an explicit post about geometry as personal philosophy. the tattoos aren't decorative, they're an operating system.
