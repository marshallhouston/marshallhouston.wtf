# the artifact

doc found 2026-04-22. filesystem date: jun 25 2011. first-year tfa teacher me, end of institute week. title: "D2D mindset into action. flow. creativity.docx"

D2D = day to day.

## what's there

block quote candidates from the doc:

> I'm struggling with translating my mindset into actions. I know that the approach I bring each morning is rooted in the empowerment of my students, but I haven't seen the results I expect from my students.

> The most difficult realization is that the results haven't happened BECAUSE of my teaching. I am learning and improving every single day, but I expect more out of myself than I've been able to give to my students. As I learn more about effective teaching, I find myself wishing that I would've implemented those strategies the day before….

> I keep coming back to the mindset versus actions thought.

> I believe that I am a creative person. I believe that I was hired because my creativity showed in my teaching.

> Yet, I will not fail. We will achieve, and I cannot wait to share these successes with the world.

> I will find a way. Together, we will achieve. No doubt.

## the meta-reflection

15 years later. still writing about the same gap.

- BFF (build friction fix) = same problem, older language. mindset is cheap. action compounds. friction is where the gap lives.
- "wishing i would've implemented those strategies the day before" → exact precursor to "you can always change it later. just ship."
- "creative person hired because creativity showed" → same story i tell about engineering now.
- the earnestness is wild. all caps WILL. exclamation. "no doubt." current-me hedges more, lowercase, fragments. but the engine is identical.

## possible angles

- the gap doesn't close. you just get better tools to live inside it.
- 24-yr-old me thought he was failing. he wasn't. the gap IS the work.
- compounding is invisible from inside the gap. only visible looking back at a 2011 docx.
- what would i tell him? probably nothing. he had to wrestle it.
- voice evolution: ALL CAPS earnestness → lowercase fragments. same beliefs, less performance.

## structural ideas

- open with the file discovery. the moment of finding it.
- block quote, react, block quote, react. let past-me speak.
- end on the meta: i'm still writing this post. that's the whole point.

## register

quiet. not triumphant. not self-congratulatory. closer to the fart-smell-detection register than the boosting-builders register. observational. a little tender. don't make it a "look how far i've come" post. make it a "look how little the core problem changes" post.

## fleshed out design (2026-04-22)

### post arc

#### 1. cold open
full 2011 doc as one long block quote. no frame. no title setup. reader drops into 22-yr-old marshall mid-wrestle.

#### 2. metadata reveal
three or four short lines. file name. save date. age. context (end of TFA institute, first classroom ahead).

D2D = day to day. worth naming. unpack the filename itself. "mindset into action. flow. creativity." the title is already the thesis.

#### 3. recognition line
"i'm still writing this." or similar. one line. white space around it.

no explanation. no pattern-naming. don't connect the dots for the reader. the artifact already did.

#### 4. close
two options, decide in draft:
- fragment about now: "still at it. different tools. same gap."
- no close. end on the last line of the 2011 quote ("I will find a way. Together, we will achieve. No doubt.") and let it hang.

lean toward no close. the 22-yr-old's "no doubt" carries more weight unanswered.

### register/energy
quiet. tender. observational. not triumphant. not "look how far i've come." closer to hiyaaa-world's arrival energy but reflective instead of announcing.

### audience
none. written for the artifact.

### voice reminders
- lowercase, fragments, periods doing structural work
- no em dashes. hyphens only.
- resist the urge to explain the pattern. trust the reader to feel it.
- don't sanitize the 2011 voice. ALL CAPS stays. exclamations stay. "No doubt" stays. the earnestness IS the artifact.
- if it could have been written by any AI, it's wrong. the whole post works because it's unmistakably marshall-at-22 and marshall-at-37 in the same frame.

### post length
short. probably under 400 words total excluding the quoted doc. the quote is most of the post.
