three frictions named during the bff post. apply bff to each of these.

## notes to words
how do i get from notes to verbal brain dump to written piece without losing fidelity? what is the kernel? what's not being lost in transition? figure out the writing system.

## finding my voice
the co-intelligence collaborative style. when do i say something exactly? what goes through summarization? what's my writing style? how to be clearer with what's a quote vs. a summarized idea vs. directness.

## ship it
the drafting process from skeleton notes to "when is this done?" am i losing the essence or making it clear? when is it ready? my pattern is side quests. set timers. just send it out.
