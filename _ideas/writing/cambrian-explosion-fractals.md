mental models for orienting in a rapidly shifting ai-augmented software engineering landscape.

## core idea

the cambrian explosion helps us understand why we should optimize for variety and experimentation rather than trying to predict the future. fractals help us see that the shape of good work is the same at every scale, from a single coding session to how an org delivers.

## why this matters

the rapid pace of change means we need grounded, reasonably rooted mental models to make sense of what's happening. without them, we either freeze or chase every new thing without learning. mental models give us a way to orient, act, and build durable understanding even when the landscape keeps shifting.

## audience takeaway

lenses for orienting and acting when the ground is shifting underneath them.
