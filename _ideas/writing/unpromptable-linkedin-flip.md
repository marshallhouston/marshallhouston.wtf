# unpromptable: linkedin formula flip

**status:** kernel / idea
**date captured:** 2026-04-22
**related:** [unpromptable kernel](../../_kernels/unpromptable.md), [lowerchaos kernel](../../_kernels/lowerchaos.md)

## the idea

flip the linkedin script on its head. follow the linkedin structure *exactly* — all the UGH-worthy patterns ("what ___ tells me about ____", the one-line-per-paragraph staircase, the humblebrag hook, the CTA at the bottom) — but the content is the case for being unpromptable. human authenticity. creativity. weirdness that no model can reproduce.

the form is the joke. the content is the point.

## the capitalization inversion

- **lowerchaos sections** = actual capitalization (normal case). the "quiet" / grounded voice.
- **"go to standard format" sections** = ALL CAPS. the LINKEDIN INFLUENCER VOICE cranked to 11.

so the reader experiences the inversion physically. the polished corporate format reads as shouting. the real voice reads as calm. HA.

## origin moment

listening to *angine de poitrine* and johnny blue skies (*meeting after midnight*). cross-genre, cross-era, cross-everything. music that sounds "wrong" in a way your brain reaches for. the exact thing david kaye writes about in [the case for being unpromptable](https://blog.davidkaye.co/p/the-case-for-being-unpromptable).

the post is a demonstration of its own thesis. a linkedin post written in a form no linkedin influencer would ship, arguing for the thing linkedin flattens.

## fleshed out design (2026-04-22)

### register/energy
full satire mode. no mask drop. whole post stays inside the linkedin-influencer voice. sincerity lands entirely through contrast between the form and the content. no quiet "real marshall" voice breaks through the bit. the ALL CAPS lives only on the site-level toggle button, not in the post body.

### audience
mixed / for myself. write it the way it wants to be written. let it find who it finds.

### argument (sincere content under the form)
the case for being unpromptable + authenticity. un-fakeable output. three musical exhibits as proof. form is delivery vehicle (joke), content is the actual point.

### structure: linear, three-exhibit listicle
"3 things [song/artist] taught me about staying unpromptable." linkedin's favorite shape. maps perfectly onto three exhibits. form-joke and content-shape align.

### post arc

#### 1. hook (linkedin trope, cold open)
- title: "what 3 songs taught me about staying unpromptable in the age of ai 🎧✨"
- opening line: "I was listening to my headphones when it hit me."
- classic humblebrag setup. commit to the bit immediately. no winking.

#### 2. the setup (staircase)
- one line per paragraph. each more self-important than the last.
- "we're all being flattened."
- "the algorithms are watching."
- "the models are catching up."
- "but there's a way out."
- "let me tell you what I learned."
- closer: "Here are 3 songs that changed how I think about authenticity 🧵"

#### 3. lesson 1: angine de poitrine
- framing: "What Angine de Poitrine taught me about ✨earning your weirdness✨"
- content: the gaps between genres. brain reaches for what it can't classify. the un-fakeable. paraphrase kaye without citing him inside the post (linkedin influencers never cite the actual source properly).

#### 4. lesson 2: johnny blue skies, mutiny after midnight
- framing: "What Johnny Blue Skies taught me about ✨refusing the lane✨"
- content: sturgill simpson invents an alter ego to make a record that doesn't fit country, doesn't fit psych, doesn't fit anywhere. the refusal is the art.

#### 5. lesson 3: fleetwood mac, "go your own way"
- framing: "What Fleetwood Mac taught me about ✨the only move that actually works✨"
- content: not subtle. not between-genres. the literal instruction. pop-famous because it's the real thing. sometimes unpromptable is just doing the obvious thing nobody's actually doing.

#### 6. CTA / close
- full influencer closer. "So here's my challenge to you:"
- "Drop your favorite unpromptable song below 👇"
- "Thoughts?"
- optional: "If this resonated, hit repost. let's normalize being weird."

#### 7. capitalization toggle (site-level, not post content)
- default = lowerchaos (actual normal-case for a linkedin post, breaking convention).
- toggle button: "NORMAL CAPITALIZATION" rendered in ALL CAPS = flips post to ALL CAPS throughout.
- alternate label: "lowerchaos" in lowercase.
- convention fully inverted. breaks the original lowerchaos post's own toggle on purpose.
