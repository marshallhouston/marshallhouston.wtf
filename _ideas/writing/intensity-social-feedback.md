## the kernel

Getting feedback as a college student from a friend: "dude, stop interviewing people when you go out."

Being baffled. "What do you mean? I'm just... this is just how I talk to people."

The intensity locked in. And knowing that that may weird some people out - they're just trying to chill and relax.

## threads to pull

- the gap between how you experience yourself and how others experience you
- intensity as a feature, not a bug - but also requiring calibration
- what does it mean to be fully present with someone in a way that reads as interrogation?
- the ADHD angle: hyperfocus on people, not just tasks
- learning to read the room without becoming someone who only reads the room
- curiosity as a superpower that needs a dial, not a switch
