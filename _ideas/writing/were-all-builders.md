the arc from feeling behind to just building, and why everyone must be building now.

## core idea

i was overwhelmed by new domains coming to my team and felt behind on ai-augmented engineering. so i started building. a system of tools (ddsearch, pup, claude code skills) to help me and my team understand our systems, respond faster, and take action.

## the arc

feeling behind -> finding a spark -> just building without over-optimizing -> getting it in front of people before it felt ready.

## why this matters

all of our roles are changing. EM roles, director roles, IC roles. the tools now exist for everyone to build in ways that weren't possible before. but what does it actually look like to put this into practice? what have we learned so far and how do we move forward? if we want a culture of builders, we need to show the real story, not the polished version.

## audience takeaway

everyone must be building. find a problem, explore it practically, learn from there, iterate forward. action brings clarity.
