prove the value motherfucker. is this valuable or fluff? specific tests, processes, changes. does this actually do anything or nah?

the gut check before shipping. the gut check before believing your own hype. the gut check on a process, a tool, a habit, a meeting, a dashboard, a framework someone handed you.

what does ptvm actually look like in practice? what's the test? what counts as proof? what do you do when the thing fails its own ptvm?

#### 3. hammer 1: the slop (minh)

> I'm really annoyed with this trend around sub-agents, and agent teams, and multi-tasking the crap out of anything and everything we can.
