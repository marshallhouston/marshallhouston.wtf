### levels (further reading structure)

- **level 0:** "what's happening and why should I care" - context-setting, big picture
- **level 1:** "ok I'm curious, show me how people are actually using this" - practitioner stories
- **level 2:** "I want to start doing this myself" - hands-on guides, patterns
- **level 3:** "this is my daily flow now" - full signal diet, who to follow, staying current
- **level 4:** "gastown's calling" - deep in the tooling, pushing edges
- **level 5:** "bonkers batshit letting it rip and guzzling tokens maximalist"
- **level 6:** "gastown citizen" - running gastown or similar infrastructure

marshall is at level 5. hasn't run gastown (yet). the post maps the trail from 0 to wherever you want to go.
