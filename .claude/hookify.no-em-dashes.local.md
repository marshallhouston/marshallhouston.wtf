**Em dashes detected in file content.**

Do not use em dashes (— or –) in this project. They feel too polished and AI-generated for Marshall's voice.

**Use instead:**
- A comma
- A period and a new sentence
- Parentheses
- "and" or "but"
- A dash with spaces: " - "
